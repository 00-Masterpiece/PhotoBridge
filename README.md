# üì∏ PhotoBridge  
**Simple photo format conversion for everyone**

PhotoBridge is a free, offline desktop application that converts folders of photos into a format of your choice while safely keeping a backup of the originals.

No accounts.  
No internet required.  
Your photos stay on your computer.

---

## ‚úÖ What PhotoBridge Does
- Converts photos in a folder to a format you choose
- Supports common image formats
- Automatically creates a backup of original photos
- Shows progress during conversion
- Designed to be easy to use, even for non-technical users

---

## üñ•Ô∏è How to Use PhotoBridge

1. Download **PhotoBridge.exe**
2. Double-click the file to open it
3. Click **Browse** and select the folder with your photos
4. Choose the output format (JPG, PNG, HEIC, etc.)
5. Click **Start Conversion**
6. Wait for the progress bar to finish

When complete, the folder containing your converted photos will open automatically.

---

## üìÇ Where Are My Photos Saved?
Inside the folder you selected, PhotoBridge creates two new folders:

- **Converted Images**  
  Contains the newly converted photos

- **Original Backup**  
  Contains untouched copies of your original photos

No original files are deleted or overwritten.
